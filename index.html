Privacy Policy

SpendSense by Heavy Data Solutions

Last updated: May 2026 Support: info@heavydatasolutions.com

This Privacy Policy explains how SpendSense (“the App”) handles information when you use it. SpendSense is provided by Heavy Data Solutions.

1. What SpendSense does

SpendSense is a personal finance application designed to help users manage and understand their finances. Features may include budgeting, expense tracking, bill management, debt tracking, analytics, financial calendar views, transaction history, and receipt-based transaction entry.

2. Information we collect and store

SpendSense may collect and store information entered by users, including:

  • income records
  • expense records
  • bill records
  • debt records
  • transaction history
  • financial categories and budgeting data
  • receipt-derived transaction information
  • app preferences and settings

This information may be stored locally on the device and/or securely stored using backend infrastructure where applicable.

3. Receipt scanning and camera access

SpendSense may allow users to:

  • capture receipt images using the device camera
  • select existing images from the device gallery
  • extract transaction-related text from receipts

Receipt processing is intended to support transaction entry and financial record management.

Structured transaction information extracted from receipts may include:

  • merchant name
  • transaction date
  • transaction amount
  • line item details

Receipt images themselves are not intended to be permanently stored by SpendSense after processing unless explicitly retained by the user outside the app.

4. How we use information

SpendSense uses collected information to:

  • display and manage financial records
  • generate budgeting summaries and analytics
  • provide dashboard and calendar views
  • support debt estimation and tracking
  • support receipt-based transaction entry
  • maintain saved user data between sessions
  • improve app stability and functionality

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal or financial information.

SpendSense does not use advertising networks that sell or profile personal financial data.

Trusted infrastructure providers may be used where necessary to support app functionality, storage, security, analytics, or crash reporting.

6. Permissions

SpendSense may request access to:

  • camera access for receipt capture
  • photo library or storage access for image uploads

These permissions are used only for app functionality.

7. Data retention

User-entered and generated financial records may remain stored until deleted by the user or removed from supported storage systems.

Users may delete financial records directly within the app where supported.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures intended to protect user information and improve application security. However, no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children’s privacy

SpendSense is not specifically directed toward children under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at the current Privacy Policy URL.
