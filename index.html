Privacy Policy

Family Budget App by Heavy Data Solutions

Last updated: 22 March 2026 Support: info@heavydatasolutions.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Family Budget App (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is provided by Heavy Data Solutions.

1. What the App does

Family Budget App helps users manage budgets, bills, debts, expenses, analytics, and receipt-based transaction entry.

2. Data we collect and store

The App may collect and store the following information entered by the user:

  • income records
  • bill records
  • debt records
  • expense records
  • transaction records
  • receipt-derived structured transaction information

This information may be stored on-device temporarily and/or stored remotely using Supabase as part of the App’s backend.

3. Receipt images and camera/photo access

The App may allow users to take a photo of a receipt using the device camera or choose an existing receipt image from the device.

Receipt images are processed locally on the user’s device for transaction extraction. Receipt images do not leave the device and are not uploaded to our backend. Only the extracted structured transaction data is stored.

4. How we use data

  • to display and manage financial records
  • to generate analytics and budgeting views
  • to support receipt-based transaction entry
  • to keep user data available across sessions where applicable

5. Data sharing

We do not sell user data.

We do not use third-party advertising or user-behaviour analytics services based on the information currently provided.

We use Supabase as backend infrastructure to store structured financial and transaction data.

6. Data retention

We retain structured user-entered and extracted transaction data until the user deletes it or it is otherwise removed from the backend.

Receipt images are processed locally and are not retained on our backend.

7. User controls and deletion

Users can delete their saved financial data within the App, including incomes, bills, debts, expenses, and transactions.

If you need help with deletion or support, you can contact us using the details below.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect stored data and use backend infrastructure intended to support secure transmission and storage. However, no system is completely secure.

9. Children

The App is not specifically directed to children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be made available at the current privacy policy URL.
